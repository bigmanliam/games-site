What is Sportle?

Sportle is a daily sports trivia game. Each round gives you a description of a sport — the rules, the equipment, the playing field — and you have to name it. You get four rounds per day, each featuring a different sport. One guess per round: you either know it or you don't.

Think You Know Sports?

From mainstream favourites to niche competitions, Sportle covers the full spectrum of athletic endeavour. Some descriptions are straightforward, while others will test even the most dedicated sports fans. Share your daily score and challenge your friends to beat it.
