What is Whodunit?

Whodunit is a daily trivia game where you identify a famous person from progressive clues. Each round begins with a vague hint and gets more specific with every wrong guess, giving you up to six chances to crack the mystery. The fewer clues you need, the better your score.

How to Play

Read the first clue and type your guess. If you are wrong, the next clue is revealed — each one a little more obvious than the last. The sixth and final clue practically gives the answer away, so true trivia experts aim to solve it in one or two. Share your emoji scorecard with friends and come back tomorrow for a new mystery person.
