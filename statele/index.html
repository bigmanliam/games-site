What is Statele?

Statele is a daily geography quiz that gives you a US city name and asks you to identify which state or territory it belongs to. You have four guesses, and after each wrong answer you get a progressive hint — the region, the first letter of the state, or its bordering states. Some cities are iconic, others might surprise you.

Did You Know?

Many US city names exist in multiple states — there are over 30 Springfields, dozens of Franklins, and Portlands on both coasts. Statele picks cities that are most strongly associated with one particular state, but you might still need the hints to nail down the trickier ones. From big metropolises to small-town gems, every day is a new challenge.
