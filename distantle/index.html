What is Distantle?

Distantle gives you two cities and asks one question: how far apart are they? Enter your estimate in kilometers and the game tells you if you need to go higher or lower. You have six guesses to land within 10% of the actual great-circle distance. It is a daily challenge that builds your intuition for how the world is laid out.

Strategy Tips

Think in terms of known reference distances. London to New York is about 5,500 km. Tokyo to Sydney is roughly 7,800 km. Knowing a few anchor distances lets you triangulate unfamiliar pairs. Also remember that distances across oceans tend to feel shorter than they are, while overland distances across continents often surprise people with how large they are.
