What is Elemently?

Elemently is a daily chemistry quiz that shows you a chemical element's name and symbol and asks you to guess its atomic number. After each wrong guess you get higher-or-lower feedback to narrow it down. You have six attempts to land on the exact number. It is a great way to brush up on the periodic table one element at a time.

Did You Know?

The periodic table contains 118 confirmed elements, from Hydrogen at number 1 all the way to Oganesson at 118. Many elements are named after places, scientists, or mythological figures. Einsteinium (99) honors Albert Einstein, Californium (98) the state where it was synthesized, and Promethium (61) the Greek titan who gave fire to humanity.
