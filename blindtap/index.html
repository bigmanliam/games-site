What is Blindtap?

Blindtap is a free daily internal clock challenge. When you click Start, the screen changes color — but there is no visible timer. You must rely entirely on your sense of time to click Stop when you believe exactly 5.000 seconds have elapsed. One attempt per day. How well do you know your own rhythm?

How to Play

Click Start and the background will shift to indicate the clock is running. Count in your head, feel the beat, or just trust your gut — then click Stop when you think five seconds are up. Your actual time and the difference from the target are revealed instantly. Share your result and return tomorrow for another shot.
