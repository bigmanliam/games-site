What is Daily-le?

Daily-le is a collection of 12+ free daily games spanning geography, science, history, language, and reflexes. Each game gives you a new puzzle at midnight your local time. Every game uses the same simple format: a handful of guesses, helpful feedback after each attempt, and a shareable emoji scorecard so you can compare with friends.

How It Works

Pick a game from the grid above and start playing. No accounts, no sign-ups, no downloads. Your progress is saved in your browser so you can come back later in the day to finish. When you are done, hit share to copy your emoji board and challenge someone else. With 12 games to play each day, you will never run out of puzzles.

Games by Category

Geography lovers can test their knowledge with Flagle (flags), Popdle (populations), Distantle (city distances), Capitdle (capital cities), and Statele (US states). Science fans have Physicsle (physics phenomena) and Elemently (periodic table). History buffs can try Datele (historical dates) and Whodunit (famous people). Linguists will enjoy Languagle (identify languages). Sports fans can play Sportle. And if you just want a quick reflex test, Reactionle and Blindtap challenge your internal clock.

Why Daily Games?

Daily games work because they build habits. A two-minute puzzle each morning adds up to genuine knowledge over weeks and months. You will start recognizing flags, placing countries on the map, recalling historical dates, and identifying languages from their script, all without sitting down to study. Share your scores with friends and the daily format keeps everyone on the same puzzle, which makes comparing results fun.
