What is Physicsle?

Physicsle is a daily physics quiz where you read a description of a well-known phenomenon and try to name it. You have four guesses, and after each wrong answer you receive a progressive hint — the first letter, then the letter count. It is a fun way to test and sharpen your knowledge of physics concepts from everyday life and beyond.

Did You Know?

Physics phenomena surround us every day. The blue sky is caused by Rayleigh scattering, mirages on hot roads come from refraction, and the satisfying crack of a whip is actually a tiny sonic boom. From black holes to kitchen magnets, the universe is full of surprising effects waiting to be named.
