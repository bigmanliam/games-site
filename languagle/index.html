What is Languagle?

Languagle is a daily language identification game. You are shown a text snippet in a mystery language and must figure out which language it is. You have four guesses, and after each wrong answer you receive a hint — the region, a characteristic feature, or the language family. How many of the world's languages can you recognize on sight?

Over 7,000 Languages

The world has thousands of living languages, each with its own script, grammar, and rhythm. Some are immediately recognizable by their unique characters, while others look deceptively similar to their neighbors. Languagle draws from dozens of languages across every continent to test your linguistic intuition.
